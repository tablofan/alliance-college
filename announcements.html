Video Recordings Must Not Exceed 1 GB

When recording videos for simulated scenarios, you must not exceed the 1 GB maximum per upload. This may mean that you need to record in two parts — that's fine, just label them clearly (e.g., Part-1.mp4, Part-2.mp4). If you're unsure how to compress an existing recording, contact your trainer.
